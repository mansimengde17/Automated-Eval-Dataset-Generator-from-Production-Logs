The eval suite that grows itself

Automated Eval Dataset Generator from Production Logs

A pipeline that mines production logs, finds the edge cases and failures, and turns them into labeled test cases automatically. The eval suite grows every night from real traffic i...

Why datasets are the real bottleneck

The hard part of evaluation is not the harness, it is the dataset. Hand curated golden sets are expensive to build and go stale the moment traffic shifts. This solves the data supply problem instead of the plumbing problem.

Why it lands interviews

It is a force multiplier that AI teams dream about: production traffic in, deduplicated and labeled test cases out, with humans reviewing only the labels the judges could not agree on.

What it turns into eval data

Retries after a bad answer, thumbs down signals, novel requests that do not cluster, and prompt injection attempts. The messy, valuable tail of real traffic becomes test cases.

Why the labels can be trusted

Each candidate is scored by multiple judge runs. Agreement means it is added automatically; disagreement routes to a human, and a cosine check keeps near duplicates out.

01
Log tail

Production logs are pulled from cloud storage every night at the scheduled run time.

02
Embed

Embeddings are computed for every request in the batch; previously seen prompts use cache.

03
Cluster

HDBSCAN groups logs by topic; noise points are flagged as high value outlier candidates.

04
Candidate select

Edge cases, failure signals, and retries are promoted to the labeling queue.

05
Multi judge

Three judge runs score each candidate; a majority vote decides the label automatically.

06
Dataset grow

High agreement cases are added immediately; disagreements go to the human review queue.

1
Log ingestor

Tails cloud storage on a schedule; handles deduplication across previous pipeline nights.

2
Embedding pipeline

Batched embedding calls with a local cache to avoid re-embedding known prompts.

3
HDBSCAN clustering

Finds natural topic groups without a preset cluster count; noise points are outlier candidates.

4
Multi judge labeling

Three independent judge runs per candidate; majority vote auto labels with high confidence.

5
Human review queue

Low agreement cases route to a lightweight annotation interface for human resolution.

6
Cosine dedup and export

Near duplicates removed by cosine threshold; dataset versioned and exported to storage.
